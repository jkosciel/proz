ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Kuba Kościelniak and I work as a full-time, Polish-native English Medical Translator with over 5 years of professional experience in the business. I am also a professional chemist, graduated from Jagiellonian University, Kraków, Poland. In May 2014, I established my own translation agency Word2, providing clients with precisely adjusted translation solutions in the field of medical translations, pharmaceutical translations and clinical studies. My usual daily turnaround of specialised translations amounts to up to 3,000 source words.

I am a ProZ certified translator, and a member of the ProZ PRO Network.

Since July 2015 I am proud to serve as a ProZ Moderator - you are more than welcome to contact me in case of any site-related issues.

As a committed ProZ user, I also strive to help other users with the terminology problems related to my areas of translation experience by an active KudoZ participation - please see details below!

So far, I received positive entries of feedback from clients and colleagues.
